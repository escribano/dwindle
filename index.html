About Us

After having children, we left the big bad city and moved to Westchester. Like you, we were soon welcomed by ConEd bills that regularly exceeded $500.

In our research, we discovered that you could potentialy lower your bill by switching to another supplier. Unfortunately, that process was filled with complexity and hidden fees. As a result, we created Dwindle to help break through the confusion.

Dwindle uses your historical ConEd data to analyze how much you might save by switching to an ESCO. We will also provide you with a list of these other providers should you decide to make the switch. The choice to do so is yours. Dwindle is free and we'll never share your email or data with anyone.
